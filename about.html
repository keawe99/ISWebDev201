About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Steven Armstrong. I am from Draper, Utah. I love sports and Information Systems.

I am a passionate individual who finds joy in an active lifestyle and connecting with loved ones. Sports and physical activities hold a special place in my heart, as they allow me to challenge myself physically and mentally while fostering a sense of teamwork and camaraderie. I relish the thrill of competition and the rush of adrenaline that accompanies it. When it comes to food, I have a particular fondness for the vibrant flavors of Hawaiian cuisine, savoring every bite of the unique dishes it offers. Fashion and clothes also capture my attention, as I appreciate the artistry and expression found in different styles. The gym serves as my sanctuary, providing a space for personal growth and self-improvement.


Above all, being with friends and family brings me immense happiness and fulfillment. Sharing laughter, creating memories, and supporting one another is truly invaluable to me. Music is a constant companion in my life, serving as a source of inspiration and emotional release. Lastly, my love for Spanish not only stems from its melodic nature but also from the rich cultures and histories it represents. Exploring the language further enables me to deepen my appreciation for the diverse world we live in.
